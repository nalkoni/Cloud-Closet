Gone the days of the closet monster. Say hello to Cloud Closet...

Cloud Closet is a simple web application that allows you to create and access closets anywhere. It liberates you so you can start your day or trip right. It is as simple as signing up, creating a closet and uploading items to get you started. You can filter your closet by different filter options, check the weather, and add to your consignment closet or your donation closet.

Cloud Closet is here to help pinpoint all closet related problems and find a solution to them.
